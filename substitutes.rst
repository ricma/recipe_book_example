Typical replacements for Ingregients
------------------------------------

List of typical replacements for recipes:

+--------------+------------------------+
| Ingredient   | Replacement            |
+==============+========================+
| milk         | soy, almond, oat milk  |
+--------------+------------------------+
| egg          | chickpea water, banana |
+--------------+------------------------+
| honey        | agave sirup            |
+--------------+------------------------+
